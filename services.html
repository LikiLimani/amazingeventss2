01 — Sparkulars

Cold Spark Fireworks

Flame-free, smoke-free, and cool to the touch — cold spark fountains deliver the drama of fireworks with none of the fire risk, making them safe for indoor ballrooms, tented receptions, and venues that don't allow traditional pyrotechnics.

  • Indoor and outdoor safe — no open flame, no smoke residue
  • Perfect for grand exits, first dances, and cake-cutting moments
  • Fully choreographed to your song and timeline
  • Operated by licensed, insured technicians on-site

02 — Dancing on the Clouds

Low-Lying Fog

A dense, ankle-to-knee-height cloud that pools across your dancefloor or lawn — turning your first dance into something that looks like it's floating above the ground. It's one of the most photographed effects in the industry for a reason.

  • Dry-ice or water-based fog, matched to your venue's ventilation
  • Hangs low and dissipates cleanly — won't fog out the whole room
  • Stunning paired with uplighting or string lights
  • Indoor and outdoor options, weather-dependent guidance included

03 — Stagecraft

Stage & Dancefloor Lighting

Lighting does more work than any other design element in the room — it's the difference between a rented hall and a space that feels built for you. We design uplighting, monogram projections, and moving-head programming synced to your reception.

  • Custom monogram or initials projected on the dancefloor
  • Uplighting matched to your color palette
  • Moving-head lighting synced to live music or DJ set
  • Full setup and teardown handled by our crew
